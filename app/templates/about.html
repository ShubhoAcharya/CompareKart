About CompareKart

CompareKart is a smart web app that helps users track price history, set alerts, and compare prices between Amazon and Flipkart. We simplify online shopping decisions by combining historical pricing data, comparison tools, and email alerts in one clean, user-friendly interface.

Features

  • 4-month price history graphs
  • Price alerts via email
  • Amazon & Flipkart product comparison
  • One-click product insights with URL input
